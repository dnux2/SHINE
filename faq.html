FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ORDERS & SHIPPING

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the Saudi Arabia and 7-14 business days for international orders. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

RETURNS & EXCHANGES

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unopened and in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service to initiate a return.

How do I exchange a product?

To exchange a product, please return the original item following our return policy and place a new order for the item you'd like instead.

Yes! All Shine products are 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals.
